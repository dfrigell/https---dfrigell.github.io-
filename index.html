About Aderio

BACKGROUND

Aderio was founded in 2018 and is a firm specialized in valuation, financial analysis and quantum in disputes.

We provide indepent opinions and reviews. Our team has extensive experience from international audit and consulting firms and are frequently engaged as independent expert witnesses.

OUR VALUE PROPOSITION

Experience and
engagement

Our engagement are always managed by persons with extensive experience. Our customers always have direct access to senior employees throughout the engagement.

Independence and
integrity

As experts in disputes, we rely on our independence and objectivity. Our work is based on facts and analyses.

Quality and
experience

We focus on what we do best. We engage in complex issues that require qualified analysis and the ability to turn facts into insight.


Our services

ENGAGEMENTS AND CLIENTS

We engage in projects with a high level of complexity that requires experience and senior involvement.

Our clients include multinational corporations, private equity firms, gouvernments and municipalities as well as small entrepreneurial firms and start-up companies.

We are engaged as experts in disputes and work with all the major law firms in Sweden


SERVICES

Independent valuation

We carry out valuations of companies, intangible assets and financial instruments for e.g. accounting, transaction and tax purposes. We assist in financial modelling.



Quantum

We give opinions in arbitration and proceedings in ordinary courts. The assignments concern, among other things, lost value, lost profit and compulsory redemption of shares.

Financial analysis

We assist clients in issues relating to shareholder value, financial analalys, incentive programs, restructuring, financial modelling and investment and business case evaluation
